# Garbage Collection Golden Principles

Use these as stable cleanup rules before repo entropy compounds.

- Keep one short top-level map and push deep detail into linked docs.
- Keep experiment status synchronized across `experiments/README.md`, `docs/plans/experiment-plan.md`, and `docs/plans/experiment-log.md`.
- Keep only artifacts that are referenced by an experiment result or an explicit follow-up note.
- Keep helper logic shared once duplicate copies appear.
- Keep placeholders out of active snapshots unless they are explicitly tracked as work items.
